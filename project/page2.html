What are we about here at STEM, no men?

  We strongly believe that the lack of representation of women, non-binary, LGBTQIA+ people STEM education and    workplaces needs to change.

What is STEM?

  Science, Technology, E ngineering and Mathmatics, the subjects that really make the world go round.

Why is it such a male dominated area?

  There are so many reasons from society seeing some jobs as a gendered roll, to lack of representation in media.    Whatever the reason, we'd like to change that!
